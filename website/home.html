Overview

Ceres?

Ceres is a website dedicated to spreading and promoting awareness to the annual Australian bushfires. We not only offer an interactive map and various live feeds, but also additional resources for people to learn more about the situation and how they can help.

Map?

Like our slogan, we believe that visualization will help people recognize and contribute to the cause. As a result, one of our main features is an interactive live map! See the where the action is taking place and earn more about how to use the map here.

Live Feeds?

To complement the map, we also have a news and Twitter feed that update automatically with relebant information! Check back often to see what’s happening that's not on the map! We hope that everyone who uses this website comes out learning something new!


Background

What Fires?

Although Australia deals with bushfires annually, the past season impacted the country greatly. From destroying homes to affecting the environment, even the economy and tourism was impacted. It may be over now, but there's no telling what the next season brings.

About Us

Reliability?

Our interactive map, which was created using Folium, uses data web scraped from NASA’s data frames. Also known as EarthData, two satellites are used to detect fire activity every one or two days! For more information, check out NASA’s FAQ here. Likewise, our charities are chosen for their contribution to the cause and we use NewsAPI to retreive articles.

Relevancy?

As mentioned previously, our map updates frequently to provide you with the latest data! The same applies to our news and Twitter feed and we hope to add more feeds in the future! Even though the bushfires may not be as relevant now, that doesn’t mean that there won’t be any news, so we aim to provide you with all that we can!
