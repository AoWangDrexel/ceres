Map Information

The map page shows the relevant areas for the Australian Wildfires.

The Red areas are where the fires are most extreme.

The Green areas are where the fires are dangerous, but not as extreme as red areas.

The Blue areas are where the fires are least dangerous and extreme, but can still be dangerous.

You can click and drag to move around the map and zoom in and out with the scroll wheel. Additionally, the bottom of the map has a slider that can be used to see the fire data at various points in time with the dates indicated at the bottom next to the slider.
